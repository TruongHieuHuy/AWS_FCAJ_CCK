### General Evaluation

**1. Work Environment**
The environment at FCAJ is truly open, professional, and heavily imbued with a high-tech culture. Team members are always willing to help untangle technical knots, even outside of official working hours. The workspace is scientifically organized, providing maximum conditions for deep work. For the program to be even better, I suggest organizing more internal Tech-talks to enhance bonding and cross-sharing of knowledge among interns.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**
I hold the utmost respect for my Mentor. Instead of just hand-holding and mechanically teaching me to code, the Mentor consistently trained me in the **"Design for Failure"** mindset – a vital principle when working with Cloud systems. The Mentor always guided me to dig deep into the Root Cause before proposing solutions, helping me develop independent engineering thinking and mature significantly. The Admin team was also incredibly responsive, ensuring all administrative workflows ran smoothly.

**3. Alignment between Work and Major**
The internship content is highly practical and serves as an excellent combination with the Information Systems foundation I learned at university. Going beyond theory, I directly engaged in building practical **Event-driven** architectures within the AWS ecosystem. This process helped me connect academic puzzle pieces into a complete technological picture, creating a solid stepping stone for my future SA/BA orientation.

**4. Opportunities for Learning & Skill Development**
The internship opened up a space for comprehensive development. I not only mastered Cloud tools but also heavily upgraded my Agile teamwork skills, technical documentation standardization, and professional communication with stakeholders. The practical lessons from the Mentor act as a guiding compass to clearly define my career development path.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**
The work culture here is extremely positive — everyone maintains absolute respect, works with high-intensity focus, but always keeps things comfortable. When the project encounters urgent tasks, the entire team is ready to "roll up their sleeves" and support one another regardless of rank. This helped me integrate very quickly and clearly feel the spirit of "Ownership".

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**
The program provides transparent allowances, flexible time support, and notably scores points by organizing in-depth internal training sessions. This is a highly practical investment for the younger generation of engineers.

### In-depth Interview

**What are you most satisfied with during your internship?**
My absolute satisfaction stems from the Mentor's guiding culture and the opportunity to directly participate in designing and operating Cloud architectures on real-world projects, rather than just stopping at reading documentation or doing simulated labs.

**What do you think the company needs to improve for future interns?**
The program could organize more thematic workshops on Soft Skills (such as Technical Solution Presentation Skills to clients) or failure-sharing case studies (Post-mortem analysis) so interns have a more multi-dimensional view of system operations.

**Would you recommend this internship to your friends? Why?**
Absolutely yes. This is a perfect launchpad for those truly serious about the profession. The professional environment, highly experienced Mentors, and real-world problems will hone both the technical and business mindsets of any student.

---

### Proposals & Desires

**Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**
I suggest adding periodic 1-on-1 Feedback Sessions between interns and Mentors every two weeks to promptly evaluate performance, adjust the roadmap, and troubleshoot technical barriers as quickly as possible.

**Do you want to continue with this program in the future?**
Certainly. I strongly desire to continue accompanying AWS in the upcoming development phases to conquer larger-scale infrastructure challenges.

**Other feedback (feel free to share):**
The internship at FCAJ is a profoundly meaningful milestone, helping me transform from a purely theoretical student into an engineer with a combat-ready mindset. I would like to send my sincere thanks to the entire FCAJ team for creating a world-class working environment and standing by me throughout this journey.
