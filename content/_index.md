### 📝 Professional Introduction

Coming from an **Information Systems** background, I have always been trained to act as a bridge between technical execution and business strategy. With a long-term career goal of becoming a **Business Analyst (BA)** or **Solution Architect (SA)**, I believe that understanding both domains is essential to delivering high-impact, practical solutions.

Despite my analytical orientation, I have a deep passion for **Cloud Engineering (CE)** and networking. My hands-on philosophy is simple: *"A great BA or SA cannot rely purely on theoretical frameworks. They must be someone who has directly configured infrastructure, troubleshot error logs, and understood system limits firsthand to design business solutions that are realistic and scalable."*

For this reason, throughout my internship journey in the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** program, every lab, project module, and community event has been analyzed and documented through a dual-perspective framework:
*   🛠️ **Technical Perspective:** Deep-diving into system mechanics, C# code optimization, DynamoDB single-table design, and AWS security architecture.
*   💡 **Analytical Perspective:** Assessing business value, cost-performance optimization, risk management, and user experience enhancement.
